Unique Dresses

When speaking of conceptual retail in Tel-Aviv, one name is bound to come up. L'etranger is considered an institution on the global fashion market while owner Shimon Zarfati, a garments designer, has been responsible for introducing some of the most exclusive labels to Israel. By collaborating with various worldwide designers and artists, L'etranger represents a refreshing alternative, offering a fine collection of cutting-edge and most modern brands, dedicated to unique individuals, both men and women, aspiring to make a fashion statement regardless to trends and tendencies. Located in a Tel Aviv's landmark building at the up-to-date urban location Neve Tzedek
